OUR HISTORY

MIMO is a company, established in 2016 by one of the graduates from University of South Carolina, Donald White. Going through the phase of moving in his college life and witnessing the need students have during their moving phase. He felt a need of some services that would benefit the students. The idea started with “what if” students are provided with options just like UBER but doing the work of U haul. This just gave a kick to develop a real-time service application which will solve the hassle and frustration of moving stuff. With just a click, now you can get help to move your stuff.

HOW IT WORKS

Any one needed help in moving either can request MIMO helper or MIMO vehicle. Minimum of one-hour charge is applied once the service is requested. You can increase number of MIMO helpers/vehicle based on your need
